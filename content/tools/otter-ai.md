### Deep Dive Analysis

Otter.ai is the essential tool for anyone who spends their day in meetings. It automatically joins your Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams calls, records them, and provides a searchable, intelligent summary within minutes.

### Key Features Analysis

- **Otter AI Chat**: Ask questions about your meetings like "What did we decide on the budget?" and get instant answers.
- **Automated Summary**: Get a concise summary of every meeting emailed to you automatically.
- **Speaker Identification**: Accurately tracks who said what, even in crowded conference rooms.

### Comparison: Otter.ai vs Fireflies.ai

While **Fireflies.ai** offers more complex workflow integrations for sales teams, **Otter.ai** is generally considered more accurate in its transcription and has a superior mobile app for recording in-person meetings.
