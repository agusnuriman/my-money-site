### Deep Dive Analysis

Claude, developed by Anthropic, is a primary competitor to ChatGPT known for its safety and exceptional reasoning capabilities. Claude 3.5 Sonnet is often considered superior for coding and generating writing that sounds more 'human' than other models.

### Key Features Analysis
- **Artifacts Interface**: Directly displays code results, websites, or diagrams on the side of the chat screen.
- **Huge Context Window**: Capable of reading thousands of pages of documents at once without losing detail.
- **Vision Capabilities**: Highly accurate in analyzing images, charts, and technical tables.

### Target Audience
Engineering/IT students, researchers, professional content writers, and software developers.

### Why We Recommend It
Claude rarely hallucinates and has a very elegant writing style. It is perfectly suited for complex academic and professional tasks.

### Pro Usage Guide
1. **Use Artifacts Mode**: When requesting code or diagrams, ensure Artifacts mode is active for real-time previews.
2. **Provide Reference Documents**: Upload your lecture PDFs and ask Claude to summarize key points likely to appear in exams.
3. **'Chain-of-Thought' Prompting**: Ask Claude to 'think step-by-step' before providing the final answer for maximum logical accuracy.
