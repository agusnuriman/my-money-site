- **Prompt-to-Video**: Input a simple sentence and get a fully edited video in minutes.

- **AI Video Editor**: Use chat-based commands to edit your video (e.g., 'change the voice to a British female').
- **Massive Media Library**: Access to 16M+ stock clips from top providers like iStock and Shutterstock.
