### Deep Dive Analysis

ResearchRabbit is a must-have tool for senior students or researchers. This AI helps you find interconnected journals through visual mapping, eliminating the need for manual searches in Google Scholar.

### Key Features Analysis
- **Discovery Graphs**: Visualization of relationships between authors and papers.
- **Personalized Recommendations**: The more papers you add, the more accurate the AI's suggestions become.
- **Zotero Integration**: Automatic synchronization with your reference management tools.

### Target Audience
Thesis/dissertation students, professors, and professional researchers.

### Why We Recommend It
This tool saves days of work during literature reviews. It is a genuine game-changer in the academic world.

### Pro Usage Guide
1. **Start with 'Seed' Papers**: Input 2-3 key journals most relevant to your topic as the AI's foundation.
2. **Use the 'Timeline' Feature**: Observe how a theory has evolved over the years through a timeline graph.
3. **Sync with Zotero**: Connect your Zotero account so newly discovered references can be used immediately while writing.
